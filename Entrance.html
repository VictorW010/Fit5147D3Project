Vaccination Progress
Background

With more than a year of the COVID-19 (coronavirus) epidemic underway, vaccines are the most powerful weapon to prevent infectious diseases. Most countries are gradually starting to set up vaccination programs to prevent the deeper damage caused by the virus. However, a certain level of vaccination coverage in the population can form an effective herd immunity barrier and interrupt the spread of Covid pneumonia. Find out more.
