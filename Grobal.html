Continents Trend

In each continent, Asia currently has the largest number of vaccines and the largest number of vaccinations, as well as the number of new vaccines per day. However, the number of vaccines per capita is the highest in North America. This may be due to the fact that Asia has a larger population base, whereas in North America, there is more health care available per person on an equal basis. In addition, Europe is in the middle of the rank and Africa almost has the smallest number of values for each attribute. it is worth mentioning that people in North America are more likely to be fully vaccinated than on other continents.
